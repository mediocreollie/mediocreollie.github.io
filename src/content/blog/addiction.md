Addiction

Understanding addiction is so hard if you haven’t had one. You may be a better person than me but I used to not understand why people don’t just stop smoking or drinking when they know how bad it is for them.

But the brain is so easy to exploit, anything that gives it dopamine will override so much of you to get what it wants. You can live with the shame, regret and fear of people knowing, worried about people’s perceptions if they knew.

In lucky, my addiction doesn’t financially ruin me but it has ruined my sex drive. If you haven’t guessed I have a porn addiction. And the scary thing about porn is it’s everywhere. And once your brain knows how to get it, it’s hard to stay away. I know telling people or getting help would help, but the ego, the ego is fragile, scared to be damaged.

I don’t deserve sympathy for this but just know if I could lose this addiction I would take that deal without hesitation. And I think a lot of addicts would.
