Learning is embarrassing

It is hard to and everyone else is much better than you. Well the people who can already do it are, but it’s easy to forget everyone who can’t.

Recently I have been trying to be more artistic, whether that’s drawing, painting, writing or editing videos. As I’m not very good and find it really cool what people can create. But it is hard, embarrassing and a skill set I don’t have. When it comes to new sports I can generally pick them up quickly, or a new recipe and because I’m “average/better than average” at some of these things I am happy to stick them, which makes it easier to stick with them, but when you see on social media all the talent and work that goes into creating something, it is easy to forget the years of practise and mistakes that allowed them to get to that point.

It’s much easier to do scrolling than it is to learn a language or watch a history YouTube video instead of studying.
