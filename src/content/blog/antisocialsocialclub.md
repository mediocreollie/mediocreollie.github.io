“Can you make social media social again?

I joined Facebook in 2013 and have posted 72 times. Instagram in 2016 and only have 18 public posts.

My mother has over 6,200 posts on Facebook since 2012, but why does this difference matter?

A lot of content on social media is now ads, AI slop, political content and influencers. It feels less and less social and more as an algorithm designed to keep you on the platform. Obviously, it works, but I’m never happy about the time I spend on the platform.

Over the past year I have tried to quit social media to no success, because the creativity and art created can’t be seen anywhere else.

So I’m going to actively try and change my relationship with social media. The latest data shows a rising decline in people posting, with only 10–30% of users posting depending on the platform.

So I’m going to start creating and posting content on platforms, not because social media needs more, but because people willing to stay social.”
