“Are there good social medias?”

Social media refers to platforms, websites and applications that enable users to create, share and exchange content.

You have your typical ones, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube etc. and the general stance is “social media bad for your mental health” which I would generally agree with, due to short form content, ads and algorithms designed to keep you on the platform.

I can’t count the hours I’ve forgotten on social media, so what social media is good?

1. WhatsApp, lets you contact friends and family openly in a stripped mode. Simple, no distractions.

Not a real social media? Fair.

2. Reddit, I have never used this social media but am forever thankful for the people that do. I cannot count the number of niche questions someone has answered.

I was playing Stardew Valley and was completing Qi’s cooking quest and wondered if I could cook bread even though someone answered this about blackberry season. I’m forever thankful for people who engage with niche topics to help others.

Screwfix forums: I use it, work for Screwfix use it. This is niche, but that’s what makes it great. Just a group of people helping others with projects.

I read a whole thread talking one night about a man going through a divorce and ending up homeless, but had a genuine community trying to help.

Google Maps: My goat.

You might wonder how this is a social network, but the business reviews are GOATED.

My partner is lactose intolerant and vegetarian, so basically vegan. Whenever we are on holiday we rely on vegan reviews when going to restaurants to judge if it is good.

If you have weird food restrictions, leave reviews, make life better for others and hope others return the favour.

The next two I would say fall into grey areas depending if you are a “hell yeah” or “hell no” kinda person.

YouTube is great.

I’ve recently jailbroken my PS3 to be able to play COD online with a new PSN account and was wondering how to get online when I couldn’t figure out how to put my SSD together. This lady was the goat. These people make it such a good platform.

But it is so big there is definitely a dark underbelly, and YouTube Shorts are just evil.

Facebook, the main part of this social media is shit.

Pure slop. I’ve seen three generations watching AI slop on this app.

But the communities are often wholesome. My grandma has reconnected with people she hasn’t seen for 40 years and some groups genuinely help people make friends.

I’ve thought for the past year I just need to quit social media, but I don’t think that’s true.

I just need to change which I use and stop engaging with the negative ones.

Honourable mention: this side of Snapchat. Never swipe into the right hellscape.
