  1. easyJet B2B Marketing Funnel


  Overview

  At easyJet, I helped build a more structured B2B marketing approach, moving
  activity from broad awareness toward clearer funnel tracking, lead
  qualification and conversion-focused decision making.


  Challenge

  B2B activity had marketing channels and events in place, but limited
  visibility of how activity translated into qualified leads or commercial
  outcomes.


  What I did

  I helped design an end-to-end B2B marketing funnel, introducing clearer KPIs
  across awareness, consideration and conversion. This included mapping campaign
  activity to business objectives, improving lead qualification and supporting
  reporting that made it easier to understand where marketing spend was creating
  value.


  I also supported a £40k trade event activation, using a gamified stand
  experience to increase engagement and generate stronger conversations with
  business travel customers.


  Impact

  The work improved visibility of B2B marketing performance, supported better
  sales alignment and helped shift focus toward activity with clearer conversion
  potential. The event generated 30 qualified enterprise leads, improving
  year-on-year lead output.


  2. AI Adoption / Change Campaign


  Overview

  I supported the rollout and adoption of internal AI tools within an
  operational environment, helping employees understand how AI could support
  day-to-day workflows rather than feel like an abstract technology project.


  Challenge

  AI tools had been introduced, but adoption was inconsistent. The opportunity
  was not just technical, it was behavioural: people needed to understand where
  the tools fitted into their work and why they were useful.


  What I did

  I helped design and deliver targeted training, workshops and internal
  communications to make AI feel practical and relevant. The focus was on real
  operational use cases, showing how AI could support tasks such as
  communication, workflow efficiency and faster problem-solving.


  I worked with stakeholders to identify opportunities, support MVP thinking and
  help connect user needs with technical delivery.


  Impact

  Adoption increased from around 20% to 80% across a 200-person contact centre
  within one month. The project helped embed AI into daily workflows and showed
  how change adoption depends on communication, relevance and trust, not just
  the tool itself.


  3. Boxing Club Social / Content Rebrand


  Overview

  While at university, I ran the Boxing Club’s Instagram and Facebook pages,
  using content to make the club feel more active, professional and accessible
  to new members.


  Challenge

  The club needed stronger social content to show what sessions were like,
  attract new members and build a clearer identity around the community.


  What I did

  I organised a photoshoot with photography students to create a bank of
  high-quality images that could be used consistently across posts. This gave
  the club stronger visual assets and helped move the content away from one-off
  updates toward a more consistent social presence.


  I used the channels to promote sessions, events and club activity, while
  making the club feel more approachable for students who may have been nervous
  about joining.


  Impact

  The improved content helped the club present itself more professionally and
  supported stronger attendance and retention, which increased by around 30%
  year-on-year during my time leading club operations.
